RUSH

Description: On my FINAL SKETCH ASSIGNMENT I created an interactive football game. You click on the screen to start the game. In the game you start on the goal line of the left end zone. You objective is to move the runner using 'a' 's' 'd' 'w' to the opposite end zone. If you run into the defenders, however, you will be tackled and lose the game.

METALLICA

Description: Our theme for this assignment was music so I created a concert. I used an image of METALLICA, as well as, images of a crowd. If you click on the screen it will "turn off the house lights" or change the background from white to black. Also, if you drag your cursor around the screen you can see that there are two drum sticks that follow.



CRAZY TACO

Description: In this sketch we had to incorporate a "for" loop in with our theme which was food. I have a taco that will move around randomly. If you click on the screen then it will change the background to a lime green color.



HAUNTED HOUSE

Description: The theme for this assignment was Halloween. I decided to create my version of a haunted house. There are random appearing triangles that pop up in the back to give that kind of strobe light effect. Also, there are two ghosts that float side to side to be spooky. If you click on the screen the background will pause and the ghosts will elongate.



FALLACY

Description: The theme for this sketch was to make a meme of a fallacy. I chose to do the argument for authority fallacy, which is the fallacy that is using the words of someone who claims to be an expert, rather than logic or evidence to support their argument. So, in my sketch I play the role of the expert of lemons and I say mine are the best because I said so. You can interact with this sketch by scrolling over the image and clicking on the image. Scrolling over the image will show the text and clicking on the image will change the background, which in turn changes the text
